What this is

Not a network.
An inner circle.

Most communities are built for founders who want investors. This one is built for a different kind of ambition — the kind that finds satisfaction in precision, execution, and building things that last.

  • Closed DinnersSmall tables curated by role and seniority. Hosted quarterly — no agenda, no pitches.

The format

Designed
for depth

Quarterly  ·  10 to 14 people

Small table. No agenda. The conversation goes where it needs to. Hosted every quarter with guests curated to the room — role, seniority, and what each person actually needs from the evening.

Monthly  ·  Member-led

Deep sessions led by members on the problems they have actually solved. No panels. No pitches. Tactical, honest, and built for people who have already heard all the frameworks.

Async  ·  Private to members

A private space to put your real problems to peers who have faced the same walls. No judgment. No attribution. The kind of honesty that is impossible in public rooms.

Always on  ·  Members only

A curated index of every member. Roles, specialisms, company stage, and what each person is open to. Built for direct relevant access — not broadcast networking.

Annual  ·  Full membership

One full day outside Mumbai. High-trust, low-agenda. The conversations that happen here are the ones people come back talking about twelve months later.

On request  ·  Warm only

When someone in the room can genuinely help, we make the introduction. No cold outreach. No noise. Every connection made through the Guild carries real context behind it.
